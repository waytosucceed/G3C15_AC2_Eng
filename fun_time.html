Fun Time : Idiom Time


Third time's a charm: This idiom suggests that if you failed at something the first two times, you'll likely succeed on the third attempt.
Sentence: After two unsuccessful job interviews, Sarah was hoping the third time would be a charm and she'd finally get an offer.

Ten out of ten: A perfect score or the highest rating possible, indicating excellence or complete satisfaction.
Sentence: The restaurant's creative dishes and impeccable service earned it a ten out of ten review from the renowned food critic.
